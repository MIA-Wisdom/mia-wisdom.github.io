Smart Real Estate Location App

PropertyRadar is an innovative mobile application designed to revolutionize how users discover and manage real estate listings using precise location data.

Download App

Key Features

Interactive Map

Uses OpenStreetMap for reliable, community-supported map tiles. Click on the map or enter address/coordinates to pinpoint property locations.

Property Listings

Browse houses, apartments, buildings for sale or rent. Post abandoned property listings with contact email.

  • For Sale - Properties available for purchase
  • For Rent - Properties available for rental
  • Abandoned - Contact email required (no owner attached)

Email Verification

Users must verify their email to add sale/rent listings. This ensures listing authenticity and accountability.

Proximity Alarm

Customizable alerts when entering a defined radius around listed properties:

  • 1 km radius
  • 5 km radius
  • 10 km radius

Note: Proximity alarm only works when device location services are enabled.

Personal Dashboard

View, edit, and track your listings including view counts. Renew listings before they expire.

Automatic Expiration

Listings expire automatically after 90 days. The system runs daily expiration checks at 2 AM to keep listings current.

Local Storage

All property listings are stored locally on your device for quick access and offline viewing.

How It Works

1

Open the App

Access Map or Property List without login - open to all users.

2

Browse Listings

View properties on the interactive map or in the property list.

3

Add Listing

Login with verified email to post sale/rent listings. Abandoned listings only need contact email.

4

Enable Alerts

Turn on proximity alarm to get notified when near properties.
